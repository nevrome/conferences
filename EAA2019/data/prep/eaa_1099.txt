CULTURAL ASTRONOMY, SKYSCAPE AND ONTOLOGY IN NEOLITHIC IRELAND
Author(s): Prendergast, Frank (Technological University Dublin)
Presentation Format: Oral

siting, a developed architecture, megalithic art and a characteristic material culture. Culturally, this sets them apart from the
