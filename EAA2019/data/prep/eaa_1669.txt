CREATING A RESEARCH FRAMEWORK AND STRATEGY FOR EARLY GOLD IN BRITAIN’S AURIFEROUS
REGIONS
Author(s): Sheridan, Alison (Dept. Scottish History & Archaeology)
Presentation Format: Oral
This presentation outlines a current AHRC-funded initiative that has created an international network of those involved in the
study of gold, to create a Research Framework and Strategy relating to gold use in Britain’s auriferous regions, 2450-800 BC.
