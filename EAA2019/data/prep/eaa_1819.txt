FORA IN MOUNTAINOUS AREAS: PROGRAMMING AND RE-FUNCTIONALIZATION OF PUBLIC SPACES IN
ROMAN CITIES
Author(s): Canino, Dario (Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona - Sapienza Università di Roma)
Presentation Format: Oral
The paper analyses the substantial operations of modification of the previous territorial or residential areas following the new
Roman foundations.
In every new Roman city, the area in which was built the forum had to be programmed since foundation. Especially in mountainous
contexts, the realization of the most important urban public space has constituted a very fertile ground for the inventiveness of
the Roman builders and for the realization of innovative architectural solutions.
