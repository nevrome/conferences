HIGH MOUNTAIN ARCHAEOLOGY: THE NEPAL TEMPLE PROJECT – ARCHAEOLOGY OF A HINDU TEMPLE
Author(s): Lange, Perry (Institut fuer Ur- und Fruehgeschichte Kiel)
Presentation Format: Oral
