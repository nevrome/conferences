RHYTHMS OF PLACE AND DWELLING: THE TELLS OF THE SOUTH-EASTERN EUROPE
Author(s): Gheorghiu, Dragos (Doctoral School National University of Arts - Bucharest)
Presentation Format: Oral
The Chalcolithic tell-settlements of the Balkan-Danube-Carpathian Chalcolithic traditions are an example of rhythmic activity of
construction and deconstruction that creates artificial forms of relief, due to the many overlapped levels of inhabitation.
