A POST-MEDIEVAL ARCHAEOLOGY IN GREECE? NOTES FROM THE MOUNTAINS
Author(s): Moudopoulos Athanasiou, Faidon (University of Sheffield)
Presentation Format: Oral
Greek post-medieval heritage is known by an other name: Ottoman. For reasons related to Nation-State identity politics, the
Ottoman era was never part of the core heritage discourse. The reasons for this exclusion have been thorroughly addressed in
theoretical terms but in practice there are only a handful (but increasing) advances when it comes to field archaeology. In contrast to systematic efforts to place the Ottoman history and built heritage in the centre of the discourse (e.g. the international
