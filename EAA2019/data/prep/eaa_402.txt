ARCHAEOLOGY AS A PROXY FOR SOCIAL COMPLEXITY. CAVE SETTINGS, PASTORALISM AND
RITUALITY DURING THE COPPER-EARLY BRONZE AGE IN NORTH-WESTERN TUSCANY
Author(s): Bilotti, Giacomo (Padua University; Centro Studi di Preistoria e Archeologia di Milano - CSP) - Metta, Christian (Pisa
University; Centro Studi di Preistoria e Archeologia di Milano - CSP)
Presentation Format: Oral
