A RE-INTERPRETATION OF THE LATE BRONZE AGE CHRONOLOGY AT BEYCESULTAN BASED ON C-14
DATING
Author(s): Kourkoulakos, Antonios (University of Münster)
Presentation Format: Oral
The excavations by James Mellaart and Seton Lloyd at Beycesultan in central western Anatolia during the 1950s revealed an
extensive settlement with continuous habitation layers from the Chalcolithic to the Late Bronze Age. Beycesultan thus became
