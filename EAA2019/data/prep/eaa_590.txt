BEYOND BRUTALIZATION AND PACIFICATION: REASSESSING VIKING AGE SOCIETY IN NORTHERN
SCOTLAND
Author(s): Cartwright, Rachel (University of Minnesota)
Presentation Format: Oral

by their baser instincts. This is a viewpoint that contemporary written sources promoted with the heathen hordes wreaking havoc on the unsuspecting Christians of Europe. However, some archaeological evidence has been used to present an alternative
