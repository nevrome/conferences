KEYNOTE. EXTRAURBAN SANCTUARIES OF EUBOEA ISLAND: STATE OF RESEARCH
Author(s): Reber, Karl (University of Lausanne; Swiss archaeological School in Greece)
Presentation Format: Oral
Euboea, the second largest island of Greece after Crete, is still not very well explored from the archaeological point of view.
This is also true for a number of extraurban sanctuaries which in most cases were known only through written and epigraphical
sources. The Greek geographer Strabon gives us a list of seven important sanctuaries, among them the sanctuaries of Apollon
