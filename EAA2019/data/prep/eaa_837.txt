LONG-TERM DEVELOPMENTS IN BRONZE AGE BURIAL CUSTOMS IN THE SOUTHERN URALS
Author(s): Schreiber, Finn (Free University Berlin)
Presentation Format: Oral
In the Southern Urals (Russia/Kazakhstan), archaeologists traditionally distinguish several Bronze age cultures. Cultures of the
middle and late Bronze Age are mainly known from numerous burial sites. However, these cultures show strong affiliations to
each other and their spatial and chronological relations are not clear.
