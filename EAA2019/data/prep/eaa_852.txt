BUILT TO BALANCE – ACHIEVING SPATIAL EQUILIBRIUM IN URBAN LAYOUTS
Author(s): Baumanova, Monika (University of West Bohemia, Pilsen)
Presentation Format: Oral
The success and failure of urban settlements ultimately depend on balance, like that of many other organisms undergoing constant change. However, the descriptive and analytical efforts in archaeological studies of past and present urbanism have often
focused on scale and intensity rather than balance – from enquiries into economy, social institutions and population density to
architectural form.
