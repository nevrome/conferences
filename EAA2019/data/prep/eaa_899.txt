GATED COMMUNITIES OF OSTIA
Author(s): Danilova, Anna (University of Bergen)
Presentation Format: Oral
Professional trade collegia were an important element of urban life in Ostia during the second and third centuries AD. They were
multi-dimensional associations, which also owned considerable proportion of urban property of various kinds. This paper, which
is a part of my PhD research project “Residential segregation in Ostia”, analyses three case studies: Caseggiato del Temistocle;
