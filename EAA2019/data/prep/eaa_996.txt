PROJECTILE POINTS AS INDIVIDUAL SIGNATURES - RETHINKING THE PIONEER COLONISATION OF
SOUTHERN SCANDINAVIA
Author(s): Pedersen, Jesper - Riede, Felix (Aarhus University)
Presentation Format: Oral
